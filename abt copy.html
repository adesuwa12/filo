Procedure To Take When Buying From FILO

1. Buyer And Seller Sign And Seal Contract Including Banking Coordinates. The Electronic Signed By Both Parties Is Considered Legally Binding And Enforceable Must Not Be Changed. The Executed SPA And Is Lodged In Their Respective Banks.

2. Buyer's Bank Shall Issue By Swift Wire Transfer To Seller's/Fiduciary Bank, A Non-Operative, Confirmed And Irrevocable Instrument (DLC/ SBLC/ BG) For The Total Cost Of Cargo.

3. Seller Loads Vessel And Gives To The Buyer All Loading Documents, Q88 And Captain's Name. Vessel's Captain Will Provide NOR Notice Of Readiness) To The Buyer To Confirm The Product.

4. Within Three (3) Business Days Two (2) & Three (3) Above, The Priority Terminal Administrator Authorizes Master Of Its Vessel To Issue A Full Confirmation To Refinery Discharge.

5. The Seller Shall Establish Full Communication And Control Between Buyer And The Captain Of The Vessel.

7. The Seller Shall Prepare The Logistics, Obtain Security Clearance And Cause Vessel Master To Proceed For Q And Q Inspection By A Nominated Agency Of The Buyer At A Preferred Location.

8. The Captain Of The Vessel Shall Issue A Confirmed ETA After A Successful Q And Q Report.

9. The Terminal Will Release Allocation In The Buyer's Name, Provide Confirmation Documentation, And Allow The Vessel To Sail To The Final Destination Within 5 Days Of Receiving The Buyer's LC.
