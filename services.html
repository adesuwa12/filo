WHAT WE DO

Trusted Global Crude Oil Delivery

Filo Petroleum Corporation Proudly Serves As An Authorized Representative Of Allocation Holders And Fiduciaries, Operating Under The Strict Supervision Of The Nigerian National Petroleum Corporation (NNPC) And The Pipeline Petroleum Marketing Company (PPMC). As A Trusted Facilitator, We Specialize In The Seamless, Secure, And Efficient Delivery Of Bonny Light Crude Oil To Refineries And Oil Companies Across The Globe.

Our Operations Are Guided By A Strong Commitment To Compliance, Transparency, And Excellence, Ensuring That Every Aspect Of The Supply Chain Meets The Highest Regulatory And Industry Standards. From Sourcing Premium-Quality Bonny Light Crude Oil To Managing Logistics And Delivery, We Prioritize Safety, Reliability, And Accountability At Every Step Of The Process.

At Filo Petroleum Corporation, We Leverage Our Expertise, Industry Knowledge, And Robust Partnerships To Provide Tailored Energy Solutions That Meet The Diverse Needs Of Our Global Clients. Our Dedication To Operational Integrity Has Earned Us A Reputation As A Dependable Partner In The Energy Sector, One That Values Long-Term Relationships Built On Trust, Consistency, And Results.

With A Focus On Delivering Superior Service And Maintaining Sustainable Energy Solutions, We Ensure Our Clients Can Confidently Rely On Us For Their Crude Oil Supply Needs. Whether Supplying Refineries, Oil Companies, Or Energy Markets, Filo Petroleum Corporation Stands As A Symbol Of Reliability, Quality, And Professionalism In The Global Petroleum Industry.
